Our Story

  • We met online and had our first date at Guthrie's Tavern--a famous board game bar in Chicago. We immediately bonded over our shared study of medieval history, love of reading and travel, and work in education.

  • To propose, Derek snuck into Emily's apartment when she was out to coffee with her friend (and Derek's confidant). He set up paper lanterns he had recreated from Emily's favorite Disney movie, Tangled.

  • When Emily came back from coffee, she found a trail of rose petals into the apartment, the lanterns, and Derek waiting with the ring!

  • We both love spending time outdoors, reading, walking, and traveling. We can't wait to spend a lifetime together, one Starbucks at a time.
